Annotate

Privacy-first document annotation. Everything happens in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Accepts PDF, DOCX, PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP and BMP. Drag & drop anywhere.

  • Draw, highlight in 6 colours, add text, sticky notes & comments
  • Save as a self-contained .html file — viewable anywhere, re-editable here
  • Autosaves locally; reopen the tab to pick up where you left off

Your documents never leave your device. There is no document-processing server, analytics, telemetry, or third-party runtime service. Work is autosaved to this browser's local storage only.
