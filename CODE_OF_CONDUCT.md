# Community conduct

Contributors and maintainers must treat one another with respect. Harassment,
discrimination, threats, deliberate exposure of private information, and sustained
disruptive behaviour are not acceptable in project spaces.

Discuss ideas and code rather than personal characteristics. Assume good intent while
remaining open to correction, make criticism specific and constructive, and respect a
request to stop an unwelcome interaction.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers may edit,
remove, reject, or temporarily or permanently restrict contributions when necessary to
protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
